About

I'm Thebe Sithole, a data visualization designer passionate about transforming complex information into clear, actionable insights. With a background in statistics and design, I bridge the gap between raw data and meaningful storytelling.

My work spans interactive dashboards, infographic design, and comprehensive data analysis across industries including healthcare, finance, and urban planning. I believe that great data visualization isn't just about making charts look pretty—it's about revealing truth and enabling better decisions.

Based in Johannesburg, South Africa, I combine technical expertise with creative vision to help organizations unlock the power of their data through compelling visual narratives.
